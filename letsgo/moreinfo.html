About Leni

Restore Trust in the Government


Leni Robredo wants to change how the government acts in the present through three ways: removing the “palakasan” or competition system, making the government follow its word to its end, and equalize the marketplace for all. To prove that she can do this, Leni Robredo's office before has received an "unqualified opinion" for three consecutive years, which is the highest audit rating to be handed bythe Commission on Audit

Boost Strength of Philippine Industries

Leni Robredo wants to improve several industries of the country for the betterment of the economy and the overall. These are the marine industry, climate industry, tech industry and manufacturing industry. She also envisions a high pay for workers of these industries as well

Revision and overhaul of flagship war on drugs

Leni Robredo seeks to find a change in how the crime of illegal drugs and the war against it is done. She wants to find and end to the “vigilante-style killings” in the war of drugs. As support to this, she once published a report outlining recommendations on how the war on drugs can be improved and be done peacefully.

Support the unemployed and out of work

Leni Robredo saw how there was a problem with poverty in the Philippines and how it is caused by unemployment. Therefore, she wanted to conduct programs for retraining and skills-matching for these unemployed people. She also wanted to conduct a Government Employment Program and also an unemployment insurance for those job hunters. She had actually started this envisioned target of hers with the founded Angat-Buhay program and Bayanihanapbuhay program who aided the unemployed and out of work.
